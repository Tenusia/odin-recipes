Lasagna

PREP TIME 35 min BAKE TIME 1 hr 30 mins SERVINGS 6 servings

INGREDIENTS

FOR THE MEAT SAUCE

  • tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef (226 grams)
  • 1/2 carrot (chopped small)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped small)
  • 1/2 onion (chopped small)
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups tomato puree (passata) (900 grams)
  • 1 1/4 cups water (295 grams)
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon basil
  • 1-2 pinches hot pepper flakes (if desired)

FOR THE WHITE SAUCE*

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • pinch salt
  • 1-2 dashes black pepper (if desired)
  • 1 3/4 cups milk (420 grams)

EXTRAS

  • 1/2 cups chopped fresh mozzarella (225-340 grams)
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese (100 grams)
  • 16 lasagna noodles** (also referred to as strips oven ready), more or less

INSTRUCTIONS

FOR THE MEAT SAUCE

  1. In a large pot add the olive oil, ground beef, chopped carrots, celery, onion, garlic and salt
  2. Sauté for approximately 10 minutes or until meat has started to brown.
  3. Add the tomato pure, water and spices
  4. Cook on medium heat until thickened (quite thick), approximately 30-60 minutes.

FOR THE WHITE SAUCE

  1. Put a medium pot over medium heat and melt the butter
  2. Add the flour and salt, whisk to combine well
  3. Add the milk slowly and stir constantly until the sauce has thickened (taste for salt)
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Pre-heat oven to 350° (180° celsius)

PUTTING IT TOGETHER

  1. In a baking dish measuring 10x10x2 (25x25x5 cm) spread 2-3 spoonfuls of meat sauce on the bottom of the pan
  2. Add a layer of lasagna noodles
  3. Continue to layer in the following order, the meat sauce, white sauce, chopped mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  4. Continue making 3-4 layers
  5. The last layer should be without the white sauce, so remember to divide the ingredients by 3 or 4, depending amount of layers

  6. Bake for approximate 20-30 minutes, or until cooked through
  7. Let sit 5-10 minutes before serving
