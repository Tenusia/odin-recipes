Salmon Pasta

PREP TIME 5 min BAKE TIME 10-12 min SERVINGS 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

    240g wholewheat fusilli
  • knob of butter
  • 1 large shallot, finely chopped
  • 140g frozen peas
  • 2 skinless salmon fillets, cut into chunks
  • 140g low-fat crème fraîche
  • ½ low-salt vegetable stock cube
  • small bunch of chives, snipped

INSTRUCTIONS

BOIL PASTA

  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil
  2. Cook the fusilli according to the pack instructions

MAKE SAUCE

  1. Heat a knob of butter in a saucepan
  2. Add the shallot and cook for 5 mins or until softened
  3. Add the peas, salmon, crème fraîche and 50ml water
  4. Crumble in the stock cube
  5. Cook for 3-4 mins until cooked through
  6. Stir in the chives and some black pepper
  7. Stir through to coat the pasta

  8. Serve in bowls
