Salmon Pasta

PREP TIME 5 min BAKE TIME 10-12 min SERVINGS 3-4 servings

INGREDIENTS

240g wholewheat fusilli knob of butter 1 large shallot, finely chopped 140g frozen peas 2 skinless salmon fillets, cut into chunks 140g low-fat crème fraîche ½ low-salt vegetable stock cube small bunch of chives, snipped

INSTRUCTIONS

STEP 1 Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the fusilli according to the pack instructions. STEP 2 Meanwhile, heat a knob of butter in a saucepan, then add the shallot and cook for 5 mins or until softened. STEP 3 Add the peas, salmon, crème fraîche and 50ml water. Crumble in the stock cube. STEP 4 Cook for 3-4 mins until cooked through, stir in the chives and some black pepper. Then stir through to coat the pasta. Serve in bowls.
