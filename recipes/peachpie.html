Peach pie

PREP TIME 35 min BAKE TIME 50 min. + cooling SERVINGS 8 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • /4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 4-1/2 cups sliced peeled peaches
  • Dough for double-crust pie
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Vanilla ice cream, optional

INSTRUCTIONS

MAKE PASTRY

  1. In a large bowl, combine sugars
  2. Add peaches and toss gently.
  3. Cover and let stand for 1 hour.
  4. On a lightly floured surface, roll one half of dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle;
  5. Transfer to a 9-in. pie plate or iron skillet.
  6. Trim even with rim.
  7. Refrigerate while preparing filling.

FILLING

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
  2. Drain peaches, reserving juice.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine cornstarch, nutmeg, cinnamon and salt; gradually stir in reserved juice.
  4. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until thickened, about 2 minutes.
  5. Remove from the heat; stir in lemon juice and butter.
  6. Gently fold in peaches.
  7. Pour into crust.

BAKING

  1. Roll remaining dough to a 1/8-in.-thick circle; cut into 1-1/2-in.-wide strips.
  2. Arrange over filling in a lattice pattern.
  3. Trim and seal strips to edge of bottom crust; flute edge.
  4. Cover edge loosely with foil.
  5. Bake 40 minutes; remove foil.
  6. Bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 10-20 minutes longer.
  7. Cool on a wire rack.

  8. If desired, serve with vanilla ice cream.
